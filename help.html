Basic Rules

Guess the hidden word in 6 tries or less.

Each guess must be a valid word with the selected number of letters.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

Tips & Strategies

  • Start with words that contain common vowels (A, E, I, O, U)
  • Use your first few guesses to eliminate as many letters as possible
  • Pay attention to letter frequency in the English language
  • Remember letters can appear more than once in a word
  • Try different word lengths using the slider for variety

Features

  • Dark Mode: Toggle for comfortable night play
  • Word Length: Adjust from 2-6 letters
  • Statistics: Track your progress over time
  • Daily Challenge: Same word for all players each day
